Rules

There are 80 mystery cards in total. They are numbered 1 to 10. There are 8 of each number. Only half of the numbers are scoring cards. The game is played in 8 rounds. Each round, each player can pick 5 cards.

Don't forget to enter your name below the score, otherwise you will be player 1 and player 2.

If you get a scoring card, the number shown on the card will be added to your score, the card will be pink. If it's a not scoring card you won't get any points and it will be black

The leading score is shown in green, the other in red. When both players have the same scores they are shown in yellow.

The probability of getting each card is updated every time a card is picked. The probability is shown under the cards.

After the last round, the player with the highest score becomes the winner.

If a tie happens rock paper scissors to decide the winner.
